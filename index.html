    Quadrant Framework ⌄
    Quadrant Framework for Workforce Value Mapping
    Reference guide: how the 12 values roll up into 4 leadership diagnostic quadrants.
    🧭 Quadrant I — Foundational Security
    “Am I materially safe and supported enough to do my job?”
    • Includes: Fair Pay, Three Meals a Day, Safety, Work-Life Balance (stability)
    • Leadership read: Survival needs dominate; limited bandwidth for higher engagement.
    • Signals: pay-related turnover, fatigue call-outs, safety incidents, overtime stress
    🤝 Quadrant II — Belonging & Team Connection
    “Do I feel part of something here?”
    • Includes: Teamwork, Communication, Kindness & Compassion, Belonging
    • Leadership read: Team climate matters; breakdowns in communication will sting.
    • Signals: interdepartment friction, handoff failures, peer conflict, low pre-shift engagement
    🚀 Quadrant III — Personal Growth & Future Orientation
    “Is there a future for me here?”
    • Includes: Growth & Opportunity, Leadership Support (development), Work-Life Balance (growth)
    • Leadership read: Workforce is ready for pathways, training, internal mobility.
    • Signals: training participation, cross-training interest, career conversations
    🏆 Quadrant IV — Respect, Voice & Emotional Intelligence
    “Do leaders truly see and value me?”
    • Includes: Respect & Appreciation, Recognition, Leadership Support (dignity)
    • Leadership read: Dignity is the leverage point; leadership behavior is being evaluated.
    • Signals: grievance tone, supervisor feedback, discretionary effort, QA attentiveness
    Why this matters: Separates baseline stability needs from higher-order dignity signals so leadership can intervene precisely.
